My Journey

As a Pharmaceutical Quality Control Executive, I ensure top-tier accuracy, compliance, and efficiency in the industry, adhering to the principles of ALCOA+ (Attributable, Legible, Contemporaneous, Original, Accurate, and the additional requirements Complete, Consistent, Enduring, and Available). My role includes managing LIMS documents and Analytical Test Methods, conducting thorough analyses on laboratory instruments, and investigating laboratory incidents to implement corrective actions. I prioritize compliance assurance, process optimization, and cross-functional collaboration to streamline operations and maintain regulatory standards. By fostering effective relationships with stakeholders and supporting key projects, I drive continuous improvement in quality control processes, aiming to transform pharmaceutical standards through innovation and meticulous attention to detail.
